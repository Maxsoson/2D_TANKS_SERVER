Welcome to Pixel Tanks Battlefront website!

This game about 2D tanks was created by two students of Cherkasy State Business College - Maksym Martynenko and Maksym Usanin. Our goal was to create a game that allows players to compete in the 80s and test their skills in driving 2D tanks at different levels of difficulty. With a creative approach and technical expertise, we bring to life an 80s game that delivers an immersive gameplay experience.
